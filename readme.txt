== Description ==

Ultimate Toggle Footer plugin allows you to insert toggle-able content at the bottom of your homepage, available for your visitors to view if they’d like. It’s great for inserting SEO content in an unobtrusive way.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire ultimate-toggle-footer folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. In your Widgets menu, simply drag the widget labeled “Ultimate Toggle Footer” into your newly available “Home Subfooter” widget area.
1. Configure the widget by choosing a title, copy, and the URLs (optional) to various other linked pages (max 12).
